### 表管理

**查看表结构**
```sql
show full columns from sys_user ;
```
**查看表注释**
```sql
select
	TABLE_NAME as 表名,
	TABLE_COMMENT as 表注释
from
	INFORMATION_SCHEMA.TABLES
where
	TABLE_SCHEMA = 'shiro_study';
```

**查看表索引**
```sql
show index from sys_user ;
```
